Pear and Walnut Salad

Description

This salad is refreshing, crunchy, salty, and sweet. Fresh bosc pears and candied walnuts add sweetness, feta adds saltiness, while slivers of red onion add a much needed crunch and spice.

This recipe was inspired by a salad I ate in Lake Tahoe, which truly changed the way I view salad. This is a great party starter and will surely be complimented by guests of all ages.

Ingredients

  • One packet of spring mix salad
  • 1/2 cup of feta crumbles
  • Two bosc pears, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of sliced red onion
  • 1 cup of candied walnuts
  • Balsamic dressing to taste

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a large salad bowl
  2. Pour balsamic dressing to taste
  3. Toss and serve immediately
