Easy Mango Mousse

Description

This easy two ingredient mango mousse is tangy, creamy, and sweet. It can be stored in the freezer or fridge, depending on how you like the consistency.

This recipe was made by me, and is inspired by Mango Lassi. It utilizes alphonso mangos, so it is truly a taste of home. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • One large tub of Cool Whip
  • Two cups of alphonso mango puree
  • Mango cubes for garnish
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Fold the Cool Whip and mango puree together
  2. Garnish with mango cubes and mint leaf
  3. Store in freezer for long term storage and for a firmer texture, or store in fridge for a lighter texture before serving
